Flutter Development FAQs

  • Can you help me to migrate my existing native app to Flutter?

    Definitely, With the help of Our expert Flutter app developers you can easily migrate your existing native app to Flutter.

  • What is the approx cost of developing a Flutter app?

    Determining the cost of development is not an easy task because there are many factors involved that make a difference. Also, it may vary based on countries or geography, domain,and business size. Other than that the development cost of your Flutter app also depends on the scope and complexity of your requirements, resources, and experience of developers.

  • How does Flutter work?

    Flutter works with single codebase functions with native APIs and services which can create native apps for both Android and iOS operating systems.

  • What makes Flutter unique for app development?

    Flutter uses its high-performing rendering engine to draw widgets and also implements most of its system in Dart programming language that developers can easily approach, read, change, replace, or remove.

  • Can the app be deployed on any of the Application Store?

    Yes, we can deploy on any android or ios app store but it is necessary to follow the norms and coding standards required for a particular app store.

  • How is the performance of the app developed in Flutter?

    Flutter apps start quickly and it runs via natively compiled code so there are no interpreters, as a result, the app performs excellently.
