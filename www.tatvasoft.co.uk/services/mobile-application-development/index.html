Mobile App Development FAQs

  • How do I know if my mobile app idea is feasible for development?

    Considering various factors like target audience, competition, and business goals, We at TatvaSoft assess the feasibility of digitizing your idea through mobile app development. You can hire our mobile app developers if you want to proceed to the development phase.

  • How much time does it take to develop a mobile app from scratch?

    Estimated time for a mobile app development process depends on the complexity of your requirements and scope of the project as well as the platform and number of mobile app developers you hire. As a mobile app development company, We analyze customer’s requirements before developing a customer roadmap based on their business goals and objectives.

  • How much does development cost to build mobile apps?

    Mobile app development process is a very complicated process and it's difficult to estimate the entire development costs without understanding the specifics of the project. For further information feel free to get in touch with us at info@tatvasoft.co.uk

  • What programming languages are your developers proficient in for mobile app development?

    As a well-known mobile app development company in the United Kingdom, our dedicated mobile app developers are proficient in Objective C, Swift, Kotlin, Java, React native, Flutter and will also suggest you best possible technology combination for your mobile application.

  • What is the difference between cross-platform vs native mobile app development?

    Cross-platform mobile app development where coding is done once, however, it can be utilized to deploy the app in iOS, Android, and Windows. With native app development, we use default language and IDE for both iOS and Android, i.e. Objective C/ Swift with xCode for iOS and JAVA/ Kotlin with Android Studio for Android.

  • Which platform should I target? Android, iOS or both?

    That totally depends on your target audience, market research and some other factors. You can contact our business development team for more guidance

  • How do you take care of UI/UX for mobile app projects?

    TavaSoft UK, being an experienced mobile app development company, always takes care of our clients' designing needs and maintain the perfect balance between the UX (User Experience) and UI (User Interface) in the mobile app.

  • What approach do you use while building a mobile app?

    Being a profound mobile app development company in the UK, we prefer an agile methodology where both the development and testing activities function in a parallel way.

  • Will your team support me to upload my mobile app on the App Stores (Google Play Store and Apple App Store)?

    Definitely! We will upload your mobile app on the App Stores including the Google App Store and Apple Play Store as a part of the mobile app development process.
